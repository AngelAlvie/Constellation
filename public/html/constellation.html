Constellations: Ordered Collections of Stars

Constellations are our method of storing stars in an ordered sequence.

Each constellation may be bookmarked so it can quickly be viewed later on the profile page.

The back button will rapidly send you back to where you came from.

Each constellation has a primary star, indicated by the halo around it. It is the best place to begin touring the constellation.
